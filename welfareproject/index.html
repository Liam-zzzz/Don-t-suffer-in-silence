About

The number of college students who take psychiatric medication has dramatically increased. These students may be at risk for negative mental health outcomes because research shows that mental illness can delay the attainment of developmental milestones critical to adulthood. This website is a safe space where students can get help on issues like sexual harassment, personal difficulties, where students can interact with the right personnel in a more up- and personal way close.
